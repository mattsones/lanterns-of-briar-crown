# Chapter 1 Character Prompts

Use these prompts as starting points for portraits and character concepts.

## Master Style Block

```text
Storybook fantasy illustration with a hand-drawn, hand-painted feel, subtle painterly texture, soft brushwork, gentle ink-like line definition, and a warm illustrated-book atmosphere. The image should feel handmade rather than slick or overtly digital. Character design should be expressive and grounded, like storybook villagers and adventurers, not too cartoony and not hyper-realistic. Use warm painterly lighting and a sincere family-friendly fantasy tone.
```

## Global Negative Prompt

```text
No photorealism, no 3D-rendered look, no glossy digital game art, no anime style, no harsh comic-book ink, no horror tone, no grimdark mood, no over-saturated neon colors, no exaggerated caricature proportions, no modern clothing, no sci-fi elements, no copyright-specific franchise resemblance.
```

# Elder Mira

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Elder Mira, an important village elder from Hearthhollow. She should appear wise, steady, warm, and quietly burdened by responsibility. Her expression should suggest both kindness and urgency. She should look like someone people trust in a crisis.

Her clothing should be simple but dignified village attire with subtle fantasy touches, practical rather than regal. Include warm Hearthhollow background hints: lantern light, cottage textures, herbs, books, village signs, or a cozy path.

She should feel like the heart of Hearthhollow: caring, serious, reassuring, and brave in a quiet way.

Use the master style block.
```

# Enna

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Enna, a sharp and capable Bramblecross watch clerk and investigator. Enna should be a Black woman or a woman with much darker skin. She should feel tough, hard, focused, and observant, not soft or grandmotherly.

She should look intelligent, organized, and slightly intense, like someone assembling clues faster than everyone around her. Include papers, pins, route notes, ledgers, a wall map, and Bramblecross watch details. Her clothing should be practical investigative fantasy attire, structured and functional.

Mood: no-nonsense, guarded, precise, and mentally tough. She should not feel like a younger version of Elder Mira.

Use the master style block.
```

# Captain Hollis

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Captain Hollis, a watch captain in Bramblecross. He should look dependable, worn, disciplined, and serious without being harsh. He carries responsibility and regret, especially around an unsolved cellar incident involving Edden.

His clothing should suggest working leadership: practical watch armor or sturdy town-guard attire, not ornate knight armor. Include Bramblecross watchhouse background details like patrol notes, ledgers, a banner, lanterns, or maps.

Mood: tired, protective, emotionally weighted, truthful, and steady.

Use the master style block.
```

# Nix Fernwhistle

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Nix Fernwhistle, a wiry road-scout connected to Lantern Road. Nix should be sharp-eyed, outdoorsy, perceptive, and a little odd. He should look like someone who reads tracks, rumors, and road signs better than most people read books.

Include practical scouting gear: hooded cloak, bow or road staff, quiver, leaf/fern details, travel-worn straps, and small route markers or lantern-road tokens. The background should suggest Lantern Road, forest paths, signposts, or hanging road lanterns.

Mood: alert, clever, roadwise, slightly mysterious, not official.

Use the master style block.
```

# Pibble Thatch


# Rowan Reedshield

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Rowan Reedshield, a sturdy defensive companion in the Bramblecross inn. Rowan should be a grounded, protective, non-fox, playable-ancestry-style character. He should feel like the strongest defensive companion option: broad, steady, calm, and hard to move.

He should have a dented shield clearly visible, practical travel clothes, a blue-green scarf or cloak, and a warm but guarded expression. He is not flashy or aggressive; he is serious about making sure people come home.

Background: warm inn light, Thorn & Tankard details, tavern wall, mug, shield, and travel gear.

Avoid fox features, animal muzzle, tall fox ears, or trickster energy.

Use the master style block.
```

# Tilda Quickstep

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Tilda Quickstep, a quick, clever, mischievous companion in the Bramblecross inn. She should feel nimble, sharp-eyed, playful, and good at turning chaos against bullies.
```

# Moss Fenmere

# Ada Willowmarket

```text
Create a half-body storybook fantasy portrait of Ada Willowmarket, a practical market owner in Bramblecross. She should be sharp, efficient, and organized, with a ledger in one hand and a rolling pin or crate stamp nearby. She runs Willow Market and cares deeply about trusted seals, accurate weights, and goods arriving when promised.

Include spice jars, dried apples, lamp oil, green Willow Market paint, crate labels, a three-leaf seal motif, and practical market clutter. Her expression should say she is kind enough to feed you and angry enough to audit a thief into the ground.

Mood: practical, brisk, clever, protective of trust.

Use the master style block.
```

# Edden Vale

```text

He should be seventeen-ish, fast, earnest, and shaken by something he encountered in the old Root Cellar. He should not look broken or horror-struck, but he should carry the weight of having heard something true that no one believed.

Mood: fragile courage, validated witness, quiet emotional weight.

Use the master style block.
```
